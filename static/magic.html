Magic

The secret to most magic is preparation and with enough preparation Joeys can do magic. They will even be able to impress their parents and friends after the meeting. Some tricks work by themselves and these are a good way to start Joeys off. Other types of trick may require a bit of practice but are well within the reach of Joeys.

Mind reading

First you can demonstrate this with the leaders. Tell the Joeys you can read someone's mind. Another leader stays with the Joeys as you go outside so you can't see or hear them. Meanwhile the other leader gets the Joeys to pick an object in the room which is the thing you will try to guess. Then you are called back in.

The other leader names random objects and you have to say if it is the chosen object or not. The big trick is that the object will be the one after a 'black' object is named. So for example the other leader might say "Is it the flag? (No) Is it the door? (No) Is it the blackboard? (No, it will be the next object) Is it the [chosen thing]? (Yes)

Do it 3 times to prove you can do it. The third time offer to turn around so you aren't looking at the other leader when they name things. Also you could use a different colour if you don't have enough black objects in the hall. This trick is also good to show around a campfire.
