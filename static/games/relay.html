Relay Games

Relays work well to tire Joeys out. Some ways to form Joeys into teams are explained here.

Kangaroo/ Joey relay

Two teams. Joeys sit down in a line with their legs straight out in front of them. Make sure there is some space between each Joey and that the Joeys have their legs together and straight. The Joey at the back of each line stands up and jumps over the other Joeys' legs until they reach the end, then they sit down with legs straight out. The new Joey at the back repeats the same procedure. Travel in this fashion to the finish line, the first team wins.

Skin the Snake

Joeys form 2 lines. They grab the left hand of the person behind them by passing their right hand through their legs. Joeys shuffle backwards with the last Joey lying down. Joeys shuffle over each person in turn as they lie down.
