One-on-one Games

One-on-one games are of limited use to most Mobs, but if you're pack is small or you have enough leaders to run more than one pair at a time, you can try incorporating them.

Chicken Fight

Korean kids play chicken fighting. Play on grass if possible. Two Joeys play at a time. The Joeys must hold their foot up near their groin with their opposite hand and stand on their other leg, eg hold your right foot with your left hand. The other hand can be used for balance. Now the Joeys must hop around and bump the other Joey over. If you let go of your foot or fall then you lose. You could have a tournament or just go through the group in pairs, more leaders means you can have more pairs running at a time. Each match is usually very short anyway, less than 30 seconds.
