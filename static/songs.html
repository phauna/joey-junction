Songs

There are a few Joey-specific songs floating around, other campfire songs are readily available from other sources.

Joey Bingo

(To the tune of B-I-N-G-O)

There were some kids who joined a Mob,

And now they're Joey Scouts.

J-O-E-Y-S,

J-O-E-Y-S,

J-O-E-Y-S,

And now they're Joey Scouts.

(Continue by substituting a clap for each letter of J-O-E-Y-S, like in B-I-N-G-O

We Are The Joeys

(This is a call and response song)

Everywhere we go,

People want to know,

Who we are,

And where we come from,

So what do we tell them?

We are the Joeys,

The mighty, mighty Joeys,

And if they can’t hear us,

We’ll sing a little louder.

(Joeys sing a little louder.)
(Repeat the verse multiple times until the Joeys are very loud, and then:)

Everywhere we go,

People want to know,

Who we are,

And where we come from,

So what do we tell them?

We are the Joeys,

The mighty, mighty Joeys,

And if they can’t hear us,

They must be deaf!
