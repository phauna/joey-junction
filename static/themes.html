Themes

Just a list of themes to give you some inspiration.

Days of the year

There are many special days that can be used as themes:

  • Chinese New Year (or almost any Asian country, changeable)
  • Australia Day (26th of January)
  • Valentine's Day (14th of February)
  • Founder's Day (BP's birthday, 22nd of February)
  • Clean Up Australia Day (first Sunday in March)
  • St. Patrick's Day (17th of March)
  • Harmony Day (21st of March)
  • April Fool's Day (1st of April)
  • Easter (sometime between the 22nd of March and the 25th of April)
  • ANZAC Day (25th of April)
  • Jamboree On The Trail (JOTT, second Saturday of May)
  • Mothers' Day (second Sunday in May)
  • World Environment Day (5th of June)
  • Queen's Birthday (second Monday in June)
  • World Scout Day (1st of August)
  • Scouts In Action Month (August)
  • Book Week (third Saturday in August)
  • Jamboree On The Air / Jamboree On The Internet (JOTA / JOTI, third weekend of October)
  • Grandparents' Day (last Sunday of October)
  • Halloween (31st of October)
  • Melbourne Cup Day (1st of November)
  • Remembrance Day (11th of November)
  • Christmas stuff (in December)

Also:

  • Summer, Spring, Winter, Autumn
  • Full moon (good for astronomy/ space theme)
  • Daylight Savings (first Sunday in October)
  • Leap Year Day (29th of February)

And some strange ones:

  • Horses' Birthday (1st of August)
  • First of Octember (Dr. Seuss book)
  • Friday the 13th
  • Star Wars Day (May the Fourth)
  • A list of weird days for every day of the year.
