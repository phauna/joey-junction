Equipment

Some suggestions for things to stock your den with.

Craft stuff

  • lead pencils
  • coloured pencils
  • marker pens/ textas/ felt tip pens
  • non-pointy kid-sized scissors, full metal blades are best
  • staplers
  • glue sticks, glue guns, PVA craft glue
  • paddle pop sticks
  • balloons

Games gear

Games equipment for a Mob need not be expensive, in fact a lot of gear you can make yourself.

  • tennis balls, soccer balls, basketball balls, ping pong balls
  • scrunched up newspaper with a bit of tape can be used as a lighter, safer 'ball', knotted up scraps of fabric also work well.
  • shorter ropes (6ft/ 3m is a good length) and a longer rope for Helicopter/ Tug of War/ etc.
  • beanbags, you can make them yourself using beans or rice
  • hula hoops of various sizes
  • safety cones, field markers
  • empty plastic bottles of various sizes, they're good for stacking, as skittles, for kicking about, etc.
  • broomsticks or other sticks
  • parachute, large tarp, old sheets, old blankets, etc.
  • rolled up newspapers with some tape are good as batons in relays or as bats for ball games
  • balloons
  • chalk
  • buckets, plastic cups, straws
